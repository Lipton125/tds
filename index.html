Methodology Snippet: CAGR (Python)


import math

def cagr(start_value: float, end_value: float, periods: int) -> float:
    """Compound Annual Growth Rate."""
    return (end_value / start_value) ** (1 / periods) - 1

print(f"CAGR: {cagr(3250, 4580, 3):.2%}")

Revenue Bridge (SQL)


SELECT segment,
       SUM(revenue) AS qtr_revenue,
       SUM(revenue) - LAG(SUM(revenue)) OVER (PARTITION BY segment ORDER BY quarter) AS qoq_change
FROM finance.revenue
WHERE quarter = '2025-Q1'
GROUP BY segment;

Key Formulas (Math)

Net Present Value (NPV):

\[ \text{NPV} = \sum_{t=1}^{T} \frac{CF_t}{(1+r)^t} - C_0 \]

Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM):

\[ \mathbb{E}[R_i] = r_f + \beta_i\big(\mathbb{E}[R_m] - r_f\big) \]

Compound Growth:

\[ \text{CAGR} = \left(\frac{V_{\text{end}}}{V_{\text{start}}}\right)^{\tfrac{1}{n}} - 1 \]

Keep r as the weighted average cost of capital for comparability when discussing NPV scenarios.
